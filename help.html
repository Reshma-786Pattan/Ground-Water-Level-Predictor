❓ Help & Support

Welcome to the Groundwater Predictor help center. Here you’ll find answers to common questions, usage guides, and contact support.

📌 Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I upload data?

Go to the Upload Data page, select your CSV file, and the system will automatically process it to generate groundwater level predictions. For analytics and reports, you can navigate through the dashboard.

2. Can I view historical groundwater trends?

Yes, visit the Historical Data page to see past groundwater levels and trends over time.

3. Where can I download reports?

After analyzing data, go to the Reports section and click Download PDF for a professional summary.

4. How can I change my login details?

You can update your name, email, password, and contact number in the Settings page.
